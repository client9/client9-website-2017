## Sans Serif

```css
font-family: -apple-system, BinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, "Helvetica Neue", sans-serif;

```

### Monospace

```css
font-family: menlo, consolas, "Roboto Mono", "Liberation Mono", "Ubuntu Mono", courier, monospace;
```
